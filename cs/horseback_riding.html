Horseback Riding

The Equestrian Program at Camp Sequoia is a great place for all levels of riders.

If you have never ridden a horse before, or you spend most afternoons, after school, at the barn, we have a horse for you! We indulge in trail rides at the Biltmore Estate, and through Dupont Forest. Campers will learn how to ride and care for the horses, and during session breaks we take groups of campers to compete and watch horse shows around camp! The barn is a huge hit for all ages, and an experience no camper wants to miss!

Riding Classes

Camp Sequoia offers the following riding classes to all campers.

  1. Horse Care
  2. Trotting
  3. Trail Riding
  4. Galloping
  5. Jumping
