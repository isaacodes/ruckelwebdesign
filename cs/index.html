A Summer Camp for Girls and Boys

Experience a magical summer at Camp Sequoia, a summer camp for girls and boys located on White Oak Mountain, in Tryon, North Carolina. Year after year, girls and boys from all over the country come to the beautiful mountains of North Carolina and Camp Sequoia – a special place where children learn important life lessons about courage, honesty,and integrity while having the time of their lives. Come discover why Camp Sequoia is A Place Like No Other!

I went to camp expecting to make friends but I ended up with family! I cannot wait to go back next year! - Julie Blanck, age 14
