Water Activities

Let's face it, summer really isn't summer until you've put on your bathing suit and gotten completely, and happily, soaked!

And Camp Sequoia is the perfect place to "Dive In!" Our own spring-fed lake provides the venue for our most popular waterfront activities, the Blob and the Wet Slide. Our beautiful pool offers a refreshing place to hang out with friends or the ideal opportunity to improve your strokes. Campers have the chance to play and learn under the qualified supervision of our amazing Red Cross certified lifeguards.

All water activities are highly supervised and participation for each group of campers is decided by the groups counselors based on availability using the following schedule.
